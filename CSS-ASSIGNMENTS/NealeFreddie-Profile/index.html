Lords of The Pestilence
- Blood Shroul -
A ghastly figure, draped in tattered, blood-soaked linens that cling to its twisted form. Its face is a grotesque blend of withered flesh and hollowed eyes, hinting at the horrors it has endured. Beneath the shroud, jagged remnants of bones and sinew peek through, as if stitched together by some dark magic. This entity embodies the very essence of decay and suffering, a haunting reminder of the relentless plague that grips the land. As it moves, a faint, chilling whisper follows, a sound that sends shivers down the spine of even the bravest souls.
- Chronicles of Chaos -
  • Location: - Cursed Catacombs - Time:- Midnight - Description: The Blood Shroud emerges from the Cursed Catacombs only when the moon reaches its zenith. At this time, its power is at its peak, making it nearly invulnerable to conventional weapons. The player must navigate the dark, winding catacombs, avoiding traps and deciphering ancient runes to reach the inner sanctum where the Blood Shroud resides. Its presence corrupts the air, causing the player to suffer a gradual loss of health over time.
  • Location: - Plague-Ridden Village - Time:- Dusk - Description: As the sun sets and shadows lengthen, the Blood Shroud haunts the outskirts of a plague-ridden village. Its presence brings a chilling mist that shrouds the village, and the player must prepare a special ward to fend off its advances before it fully manifests and consumes the village.
  • Location: - Forest of Woe - Time:- Dawn - Description: Just before the first light touches the cursed woods, the Blood Shroud appears in the Forest of Woe, where it feeds on the souls of the lost. The player must confront it during this fleeting window, using the forest's natural barriers and enchanted flora to weaken its defenses. However, the forest itself twists under the Blood Shroud’s influence, with trees turning against the player, making this encounter both a physical and mental challenge.

Game Synopsis

Pestilence

A Plague Reborn

Action/RPG

In a kingdom ravaged by a mysterious and malevolent force known as the Shroud, hope has become a distant memory. Once thriving villages now stand as hollow shells, their people consumed by despair, and the land itself twisted by a darkness that seems to have no end. At the heart of this chaos lies a prophecy—one that foretells the coming of a plague doctor, destined to confront the Shroud and its enigmatic master, The Raven.

Pestilence is a dark, immersive RPG where every decision shapes the fate of a world teetering on the brink of oblivion. As a plague doctor in training, you are thrust into a battle not only against the Shroud but against time itself. Guided by an ancient prophecy, you must navigate a kingdom in ruins, forging alliances with desperate villagers, unraveling the mysteries of a forgotten past, and facing the relentless spread of the Shroud that seeks to consume all.

Chapter

The prophecy is intricately tied to the very fabric of time. Every 444 years, a new prophecy cycle begins, resetting the world while retaining the echoes of your past deeds. Each cycle, known as a Prophecy Tier, introduces new challenges, deeper mysteries, and powerful enemies. As the in-game clock ticks forward, the time of day and day of the week become crucial, with specific events and opportunities arising only at certain moments. From the quiet morning of Sun’s Dawn to the foreboding night of Shadow’s Veil, time is both your ally and your greatest adversary.

Your journey will take you through decaying villages, haunted forests, and ancient ruins as you confront The Raven, a figure shrouded in mystery and darkness. With each passing day, the Shroud’s influence grows, forcing you to make difficult choices: Will you sacrifice your own humanity to save the kingdom, or will you succumb to the same darkness that plagues the land? The answers lie within the cycles of prophecy, where every 444 years, a new path is forged, and a new destiny awaits.

Pestilence is more than a game—it is a saga of survival, sacrifice, and the ceaseless struggle against the encroaching darkness. Will you break the cycle and banish the Shroud forever, or will you become another lost soul in its endless tide? The fate of the kingdom rests in your hands, bound by the threads of prophecy and the relentless march of time.
